Electronics & Robotics
Club of Adamjee - ERA

The Electronics and Robotics Club of Adamjee (ERA), established on September 19, 2019, by a Senior ICT Teacher, focuses on educating students in robotics and creating impactful robots. It has grown into one of the largest clubs at Adamjee Cantonment Public School, promoting innovation, teamwork, and technical skills through various activities.

Goal

The goal of ERA is to inspire innovation and teamwork through hands-on learning in robotics and electronics. It helps members develop problem-solving skills, creativity, and technical expertise while participating in projects, competitions, and research to prepare for future technological advancements.
